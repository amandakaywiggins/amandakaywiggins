Amanda has a BA in English and Art and MA in Theater and Performance Studies. She learned full-stack development to bring those creative skills into these web-dominated times.

She knows design, creativity, and how to finish a project. She can think on her feet and likes building on the works of others both collaboratively and individually.

In her spare time, Amanda likes boxing, knitting, and playing with dogs. She likes to work with her hands and to read long novels. Unsuprisingly, Herman Melville's Moby-Dick is her favourite book. Her top five favourite foods are tacos, pizza, cannoli, bread, and tacos (at the moment, y'all, because she really loves food of all kinds). Sometimes she uses British spellings because she lived in England for two years. One day she wants to have a wife and a yard so she can actually garden.

Mostly, Amanda wants to always keep learning and growing. And also to get really really good at coding.
